## Applications of Computer Vision
Computer vision has a wide range of applications and has had a significant impact in many fields. Some common applications of computer vision include:

1. **Image and video recognition:** Computer vision has been used to develop systems that can analyze and classify images and videos, allowing them to identify objects, people, and other features in the data.
2. **Autonomous vehicles:** Computer vision has been used to enable autonomous vehicles, such as self-driving cars and drones, to navigate and make decisions based on their surroundings.
3. **Medical image analysis:** Computer vision has been used to analyze medical images, such as X-rays and CT scans, to assist with diagnosis and treatment planning.
4. **Retail:** Computer vision has been used in retail for tasks such as product recognition and customer tracking.
5. **Manufacturing:** Computer vision has been used in manufacturing to automate tasks such as quality control and defect detection.
6. **Agriculture:** Computer vision has been used in agriculture to automate tasks such as crop monitoring and yield prediction.
7. **Surveillance:** Computer vision has been used in surveillance systems to identify and track individuals or objects in real-time.

These are just a few examples of the many applications of computer vision. The use of computer vision is constantly evolving and expanding as new advances are made in the field.
